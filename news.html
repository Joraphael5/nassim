N-Power Batch C

All N-Power batch-c applicants are expected to update their profile and take
test for possible shortlisting to the next stage of the N-Power application
process

Please note that all successful candidates will be sent a mail and given
directions on the next stage.

Candidates not selected can re-apply for the next batch of the programme
as soon as it opens for application.

Signed NASIMS

Test Portal



Instructions:

Please make sure your records are fully updated on the NASIMS portal
before taking this test

For first time login, recover your password using the underlisted
procedures:

  1. Click on login
  2. Use the "Forgot Password" Option
  3. Select either N-POWER application email or N-POWER application ID
  4. Enter the information of the option selected
  5. Click on “Reset Password”
  6. Enter your new password and click proceed
  7. Login with your N-POWER application email or N-POWER application ID and newly
    created password

After successful reset of password, please login to update your records before taking test.

Note:

This test is time base and you are expected to answer 20 questions in 10
mins.

  1. Test cannot be retaken after submission
  2. You cannot skip any question
  3. Every question is allotted equal score
  4. f you are timed out after the third attempt without submitting, you will not be
    able to take the test again.
